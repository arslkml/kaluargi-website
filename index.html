ABOUT US

Established in 2018, Makmur Jaya Coffee is a reputable coffee company founded in Bandung. Our commitment to quality and excellence has garnered us a loyal customer base and recognition in the coffee industry

At Makmur Jaya Coffee, we firmly believe in the power of dreams and how they can drive meaningful change. Our vision is to make all those involved in our journey more prosperous and elevate our overall quality of life through our work. Every revolution starts with a dream, and we aspire to be at the forefront of shaping the future of the coffee industry. Kaluargi is a cozy and warm space to gather with friends and family.

To achieve our vision, we have set forth a mission that revolves around nurturing our team to go the extra mile, supporting the growth of our beloved industry, and creating a greater impact on our society. We believe that fostering inclusivity, promoting growth, and upholding integrity are core values within our organization.
